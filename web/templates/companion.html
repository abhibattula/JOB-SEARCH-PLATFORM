Connect Job Engine to your browser

Apply Assist can fill applications right in your own Chrome or Edge — where you're already signed in to job sites — instead of a separate window. This is a one-time, $0 setup.

Checking connection…
  1. Open your browser's extensions page: paste chrome://extensions (Chrome) or edge://extensions (Edge) into the address bar.
  2. Turn on Developer mode (top-right toggle).
  3. Click Load unpacked and choose this exact folder:
    {{ ext_path }}
  4. That's it — the status above turns green within a few seconds. You only do this once; app updates keep the companion current automatically.
Windows shows "unknown publisher" — is that a problem?

No. The app isn't code-signed yet (that costs money and the app is free), so Windows SmartScreen shows a one-time "unrecognized app" notice on the installer — click More info → Run anyway. The browser companion itself loads locally and never leaves your machine.

Prefer not to install anything? Apply Assist still works without the companion — it opens a separate assistant browser window and fills there, exactly as before.
